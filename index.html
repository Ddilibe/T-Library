A rich collection of resources

We offer a diverse collection of print and electronic materials, including
books, journals, articles, ebooks, databases, and multimedia
resources. Our collection is constantly expanding
to meet the evolving needs of our users.

Expert assistance

Our friendly and knowledgeable librarians are always happy to assist
you with research, information retrieval, and navigating
our resources. They can help you with reference
questions, citation management, and more.

Modern facilities and Technology resources

Our library offers a variety of comfortable and well-equipped spaces
for studying, collaborating, and accessing technology. We provide
access to a wide range of technology resources, including
computers, printers, scanners, and Wi-Fi.

Thermac Library

Welcome to the Thermac Library, your one-stop shop for all your academic and research needs!

We are a vibrant and dynamic library committed to providing students, and staff of University of Nigeria, Nsukka, Mechanical Engineering Department with access to a vast collection of scholarly resources and information services. Our mission is to support your academic success and research endeavors by fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment that encourages learning, discovery, and collaboration.
